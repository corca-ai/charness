# Effectuation

This reference captures the effectuation moves that matter to `ideation` when
uncertainty is high.

## Core Moves

- start from available means: who we are, what we know, and whom we know
- optimize for affordable loss before expected-return math when evidence is
  still thin
- treat early stakeholder commitments as shaping the opportunity, not merely
  validating a fixed plan
- use surprises and constraints as inputs that can redirect the concept

## Do Not Overclaim

- effectuation is not a ban on planning
- it is a shaping lens for uncertainty, not a substitute for later `spec`
- once the concept stabilizes, move back to clearer contract and sequencing work

# Interview Migration

`ideation` absorbs the useful core of `interview` and then goes further.

## Keep

From `interview`, keep:

- restating the problem in concrete terms
- separating facts, assumptions, and open questions
- gathering the smallest context that reduces uncertainty
- ending with a sharper execution frame

## Add

`ideation` adds:

- stronger concept shaping instead of pure discovery
- premise challenge and alternative framing
- optional entity/stage separation when it clarifies the design
- problem framing questions such as status quo, wedge, and demand reality

## Deprecation Direction

- treat `interview` as absorbed into `ideation`
- future migrations should route discovery-before-spec requests to `ideation`
- do not preserve a separate public `interview` surface in `charness`

### Requested facts (now in the asset)
- **`create_task(coro, *, name, context, eager_start, **kwargs)`** — schedules on `get_running_loop()`, `RuntimeError` with no running loop; keep a reference to avoid GC.
- **`gather(*aws, return_exceptions=False)`** — results in `aws` order; default propagates the first exception but **leaves siblings running**; `return_exceptions=True` collects them; **cancelling `gather` cancels all pending children**.
- **`wait_for(aw, timeout)`** — on timeout **cancels the task and raises `TimeoutError`**; may exceed `timeout` (waits for cancellation); `shield()` to protect the inner task. Contrast: `wait(...)` does *not* raise on timeout (returns `pending`).
- **`timeout(delay)`** async CM — converts the injected `CancelledError` into `TimeoutError` at its boundary.
- **Cancellation** — `Task.cancel(msg)` injects `CancelledError` (no guarantee; can be suppressed with `uncancel()`); `CancelledError` subclasses `BaseException` (won't be caught by `except Exception`); `shield()` is the opt-out boundary.

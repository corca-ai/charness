## Critical Decisions

- **Ground-truth before writing kills (the #219 discipline), applied to both
  #219 and #261.** Per-mutant apply-then-test classification separated live-real
  from equivalent/already-killed before any test was written. This was the
  highest-leverage call: it surfaced that #219's dominant survivor class was
  already filtered by `a0b8de0`, and that most of #261's named survivors are
  equivalent-by-construction — avoiding wasted kill attempts and false claims.
- **#219 closeable on current main.** Discovering `a0b8de0` (post-#219, citing
  #219) filters the equivalent annotation-union half turned an apparently-stale
  66.1% regression into a clean close once the 5 real survivors were killed.
- **#261 bounded + follow-up (#265), operator-chosen.** The trio passes the gate
  (85–89%); bounding the hardening and filing the exhaustive triage avoided
  ballooning a hardening task. Asked the operator at the 2/3-done boundary
  rather than assuming.
- **Declared, not dodged.** The `disabled` attention-state in the #262 runner
  was declared in the visibility manifest (#175 gate) rather than reworded to
  slip the scanner.
